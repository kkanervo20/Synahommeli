ModulationSource

class ModulationSource

Source of the modulating signal.

Members:

EXTERNAL : The carrier waveform is modulated with an external waveform.

INTERNAL : The carrier waveform is modulated with an internal waveform. This is default Modulation Source.

CHANNEL1 : The source signal for AM will be the channel CH1. CH1 may only be selected as a modulation source for Channel 2 of a 2 channel instrument.

CHANNEL2 : The source signal for AM will be the channel CH2. CH2 may only be selected as a modulation source for Channel 1 of a 1 channel instrument.
